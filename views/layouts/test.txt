Proofreader applicants are tested primarily on their spelling, speed, and skill in finding errors in the sample text. 
Toward that end, they may be given a list of ten or twenty classically difficult words and a proofreading test, both tightly timed. 
The proofreading Test Will Often Have a Maximum number of errors per quantity of text and a minimum amount of time to find them.
